Our Self Defence Class

Nolo Mphele is your vibrant, energetic instructor. Her classes fill the gym with energy you need.

Our Self-defense classes include comprehensive training designed to equip women with the skills and confidence needed to protect themselves in potentially dangerous situations. These classes typically combine physical techniques, mental preparedness, and situational awareness to ensure women can respond effectively to threats. The curriculum often includes a mix of martial arts, such as Krav Maga, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, and basic boxing. These disciplines are chosen for their practicality in real-world situations, focusing on strikes, grappling, defensive maneuvers, and escape techniques.


For women, self-defense classes offer unique advantages. Statistically, women are more likely to face certain types of assaults, and having the skills to defend themselves can be empowering. These classes create a safe and supportive environment where women can learn without judgment or intimidation. In addition to physical techniques, instructors often cover important topics like recognizing potential threats, verbal de-escalation strategies, and understanding the psychology of an attacker. This holistic approach ensures that women are not only physically prepared but also mentally ready to handle various situations.
